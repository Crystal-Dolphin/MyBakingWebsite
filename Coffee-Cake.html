Coffee Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 3 Tablespoons Instant Coffee
  • 1/2 Cup Buttermilk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla

Recipe:

  1. Preaheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix suagr, flour, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Melt 1 cup of butter. While that is melting add instant coffee to 1 cup of boiling water.
  4. Mix together coffee mixture and butter. Let boil for 10 seconds then set aside.
  5. In a separate bowl, add buttermilk, eggs, baking soda, and vanilla.
  6. Pour the butter/coffee mixture into the flour mixture. Stir the mixture together gently. The purpose of this is to cool the mixture down, not to combine everything.
  7. Add in the egg mixture and stir gently until well combined.
  8. Pour batter into well greased pans.
  9. Bake for 20 - 30 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
