Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

 
  • 3/4 cup (1 1/2 sticks) butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Any other ingredients for different flavorings
 

Recipe:

 
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° and line two cookie sheets with parchment.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes. Mix in egg and vanilla.
  3. Whisk together flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Then add mixture to wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  5. Place tablespoon balls of dough onto parchment-lined cookie sheets. Press down lightly on each cookie to flatten slightly.
  6. Bake until edges are just starting to brown, 9 to 10 minutes.
