Mapple Apple Spice Cake

Ingredients:

 
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice
  • 3 cups chopped peeled apples
 

Recipe:

 
  1. Preheat oven to 350° and grease and flour cake pan.
  2. In large bowl, combine sugar, brown sugar and 3/4 cup butter; beat until smooth. Add 3/4 cup syrup and eggs; blend well.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, nutmeg and allspice; mix well.
  4. Then add mixture to wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  5. Stir in apples and 1/2 cup walnuts.
  6. Bake at 350°F. for 55 to 65 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
