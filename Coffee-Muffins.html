Coffee Muffins

Muffin Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Eggs

Glaze Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup confectionersâ€™ Sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon Milk

Recipe:

  1. Preaheat oven to 375 degrees. Add liners to mufffin tin.
  2. Combine flour, sugar,baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk together milk, canola oil and egg.
  4. Pour mixture over dry ingredients and stir using a rubber spatula.
  5. Pour batter evenly into muffin tin.
  6. Bake for 15 - 20 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  7. For glaze, whisk together sugar, vanilla, and milk until smooth. Add more sugar as needed to thicken glaze.
  8. Glaze muffins once cool.
