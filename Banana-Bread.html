Banana Bread

Ingredients:

 
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 1/8 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Buttermilk or Milk
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 3 Mashed Bananas
  • Chopped Walnuts or Chocolate Chips(optional)
 

Recipe:

 
  1. Preaheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Add flour, baking soda, and and salt to a small bowl.
  3. Mix egg, sugar, and oil until well combined.
  4. Mix together wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Add milk, vanilla, and bananas into mixture until well incorperated.
  6. Mix in other add-ins such as walnuts.
  7. Pour batter into a well greased pan.
  8. Cook for 20 - 30 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
