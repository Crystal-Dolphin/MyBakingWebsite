Carrot Cake

Ingredients:

 
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Soda
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3 Teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
  • 3 Cups Grated Carrots
 

Recipe:

 
  1. Preaheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Beat together eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl.
  3. Add in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. Stir in carrots.
  5. Pour in to a well greased pan.
  6. Cook for 30 - 50 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
